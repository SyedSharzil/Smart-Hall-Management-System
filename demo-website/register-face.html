📋 Capture Guidelines

✅ GOOD CAPTURE

😊
  • Face directly facing camera
  • Good, even lighting
  • Neutral expression
  • Eyes clearly visible
  • No glasses/mask
  • Plain background
  • Distance: 2-3 feet

❌ BAD CAPTURE

😎
  • Face turned sideways
  • Too dark/bright
  • Sunglasses worn
  • Hair covering face
  • Multiple people
  • Blurry/moving
  • Too close/far
