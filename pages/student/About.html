What is Smart Hall?

Smart Hall is an intelligent, web-based hall management system designed to digitize and streamline every aspect of residential university hall operations. Built with modern web technologies and Firebase cloud infrastructure, it provides a seamless experience for both students and administrators.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate the inefficiencies of traditional paper-based hall management. From room allocation to meal tracking, attendance monitoring to complaint resolution — Smart Hall brings everything under one unified digital platform that is accessible from any device, anytime.

Key Capabilities

Smart Hall covers the full spectrum of residential hall operations: student registration and profile management, intelligent room allocation with real-time seat availability, meal management with calendar-based scheduling, guest room booking, digital complaint submission and tracking, notice and document management, GPS-based attendance verification, payment tracking, and emergency SOS features.
